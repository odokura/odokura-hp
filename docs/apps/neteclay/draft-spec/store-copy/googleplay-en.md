## Full description

```
<b>[From the rhythm of your arms to a quiet sleep]</b>
NeteClay is a music app that supports settling your child to sleep.
It begins at a fairly quick tempo that matches cuddling and patting, then gradually slows over time.
After your child falls asleep, it keeps a low, quiet sound to watch over them, and once sleep deepens it fades out and stops on its own.

<b>[Made so tired parents can take it easy]</b>
Tap the large button on the home screen once, and it runs and stops automatically.
No age input, no volume setting, no preset picking. Fine-tuning is optional, only on the nights you want it.

<b>[Main features]</b>
✅ <b>One-tap start</b> (a large play button, easy one-handed while holding your child)
✅ <b>Sound that settles</b> (tempo eases down; a low sound supports the deepening phase)
✅ <b>Soothe again</b> (gently returns toward the intro when your child stirs)
✅ <b>Auto / manual mode</b> (hands-free, or tap "Fell asleep" to move into deepening)
✅ <b>Timeline adjustment</b> (feel out when sleep comes and deepens; optional)
✅ <b>Low-stimulation screen</b> (not glaring in a dark room; no pop-ups during bedtime)

<b>[Safe to use]</b>
No microphone, sensors, or location. No server; all settings and records are stored only on your device.
Works in airplane mode (except purchase and restore).

<b>[About pricing]</b>
The core bedtime features are all free. Paid content is only a one-time sound pack purchase; there is no subscription.
Features tied to falling asleep are never locked behind payment.

<b>[Please note]</b>
NeteClay supports a bedtime environment with sound and is not for medical or therapeutic use.
It does not measure or diagnose sleep, and does not guarantee your child's sleep.
If you have concerns about your child's sleep or development, please consult a professional.
```
