## Description

```
[From the rhythm of your arms to a quiet sleep]
NeteClay is a music app that supports settling your child to sleep.
It begins at a fairly quick tempo that matches cuddling and patting, then gradually slows down over time.
After your child falls asleep, it keeps a low, quiet sound to watch over them, and once sleep deepens it fades out and stops on its own.
Both the start and the end are gradual, so a dozing child is less likely to be startled.

[Made so tired parents can take it easy]
The default path is designed for a parent holding a child while half-asleep.
Tap the large button on the home screen once, and it runs and stops automatically.
No age input, no volume setting, no preset picking. Fine-tuning is optional, only on the nights you want it.

[Main features]
- One-tap start with a large play button, easy to reach one-handed.
- Tempo eases down over time; a low sound supports the deepening phase.
- "Soothe again" gently returns toward the intro when your child stirs.
- Auto mode that completes hands-free, or manual mode where you tap "Fell asleep".
- Optional timeline adjustment for when sleep comes and deepens.
- A low-stimulation screen that is not glaring in a dark room. No pop-ups during bedtime.

[Safe to use]
- No microphone, sensors, or location.
- No server; all settings and records are stored only on your device.
- Works in airplane mode (except purchase and restore).

[About pricing]
The core bedtime features (variable-tempo playback, auto stop, timeline adjustment, records) are all free.
Paid content is only a one-time sound pack purchase; there is no subscription.
Features tied to falling asleep are never locked behind payment.

[Please note]
NeteClay supports a bedtime environment with sound and is not intended for medical or therapeutic use.
It does not measure or diagnose sleep, and does not guarantee your child's sleep.
If you have concerns about your child's sleep or development, please consult a professional.
```
