## Title
Hatsu-go: Toddler Speech Log

## Short Description
See your toddler’s world through speech logs, early learning, and video memories.

## Full Description
<b>Track their words, watch their world.</b>
Hatsu-go is an app that helps you record your child’s growing vocabulary and how much they understand. It lets you keep track of how your child’s understanding grows and how the way they see the world changes.
The joy and surprise of your baby’s first words. New words that keep growing. Funny little mistakes you can only hear right now. And a clear “Mama” or “Dada.”
With Hatsu-go, you can record each word with video along a timeline. As your child’s words grow, you can also look back on the moment, the situation, and how it felt.

<b>A Speech and Growth Log Made by a Parent</b>
The developer is also a parent of a daughter with developmental needs.
Because of this, the app lets you record each word in detail, including whether it was spoken on their own, imitated, or understood, along with the situation in which it was spoken.
The monthly summary and chart features let you see at a glance when a word was first used, in what situations, and how it grew over time.
Of course, it is also useful for families of neurotypical children. It is designed to help parents explain their child’s progress more easily and clearly during well-child visits or developmental evaluations.
The core features that support your parenting, like logging and charts, are completely free and unlimited to use.

<b>Key Features</b>
✅ <b>Speech Logging</b> (Record date, word, understanding, context notes, and videos)
✅ <b>Charts and Visualization</b> (See vocabulary count and understanding in monthly charts)
✅ <b>Summaries and Image Saving</b> (Save and share vocabulary summaries as images)
✅ <b>History and List View</b> (Sort and search the history and progress of saved words)
✅ <b>Secure Backup</b> (Premium feature)
✅ <b>Cross-Device and OS Data Transfer</b> (Premium feature)
✅ <b>Unlimited Video Recording</b> (Premium feature)

<b>Secure Privacy and Buy-Once Premium</b>
Your child’s precious speech data and videos are never uploaded to external servers. They are securely saved on your device only.
When you change devices, the Premium feature lets you safely move your data with encrypted backup and data transfer.
<b>The Premium feature is a one-time purchase for lifetime use. No subscription needed.</b>

<b>Disclaimer and Important Notice</b>
<b>Hatsu-go</b> is a management tool designed for parents to record their child’s speech development.
This app does not provide medical diagnosis, professional advice, or treatment plans. It is not a substitute for professional evaluation.
If you have any concerns regarding your child’s speech, hearing, or overall development, please consult a qualified professional such as a pediatrician or speech-language pathologist.
